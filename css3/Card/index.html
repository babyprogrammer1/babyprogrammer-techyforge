Pink Flamingo

Pink flamingos are wading birds known for their vibrant pink feathers, long legs, and graceful necks.

Leopard

Leopards are large cats known for their golden, spotted coats and remarkable agility, making them excellent climbers and hunters.

Tiger

Tigers are the largest wild cats in the world, known for their striking orange coats with black stripes and powerful build.

Giraffe

Giraffes are the tallest land animals, known for their long necks, unique spotted patterns, and gentle demeanor.

Squirrel

Squirrels are small rodents known for their bushy tails, agility, and habit of storing nuts for the winter.

Fox

Foxes are small to medium-sized omnivorous mammals known for their bushy tails, pointed ears, and cunning behavior.
